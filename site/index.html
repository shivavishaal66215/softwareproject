Invigilation
Management System
Exams are a vital part of every educational system out there, from kindergarten to a Doctorate. And one of the most significant roles in an exam environment is that of an Invigilator, the one who oversees the process. Usually, invigilator scheduling is done by hand, by a person who goes through the exam timetable and the faculty timetables and computes. But to ensure maximum efficiency, we've introduced algorithms and software into the picture. Our Invigilator Scheduling System is as flexible as human nature. Is an invigilator caught up in something? There's a sudden change in the exam timetable? Gotta notify the faculty immediately about the change in the schedule? We've got you covered. This is just the tip of the iceberg of what this system can do. We strive for maximum efficiency, minimal chaos, and zero errors.
